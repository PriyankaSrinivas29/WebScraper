Among its key achievements the Department of Health Research (DHR) has enlisted creation of Med-Tech Mitra, a platform to help start-ups and private players by assisting in securing clinical validation and upscaling services; Implementation of National One Health Mission for Pandemic Preparedness; upgrading six Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDLs) into Integrated Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (IRDLs) to cover larger domain of infectious diseases.
